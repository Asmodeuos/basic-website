Mythical creature definitions

Dragon
A large, frightening imaginary animal, often represented with wings, a long tail, and fire coming out of its mouth
Phoenix
A bird in Egyptian mythology that lived in the desert for 500 years and then consumed itself by fire, later to rise renewed from its ashes
Vampire
In popular folklore, an undead being in human form that survives by sucking the blood of living people, especially at night
Werewolf
A person believed to have been transformed into a wolf or to be capable of assuming the form of a wolf
